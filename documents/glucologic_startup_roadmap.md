### What to Measure

## 🏥 Phase 3 — Clinical Validation (Month 3–6)

> This is what separates a toy app from a real medical product.

### Steps
1. **Partner with one hospital or clinic** — offer free pilot
2. Recruit **50 Type 2 diabetic patients** under a doctor's supervision
3. Run a **3-month observational study**:
   - Track: Glucose logs, suggestions given, actual doses taken, outcomes
4. Measure: Did users who followed suggestions show better HbA1c?
5. Write a **white paper / case study** from the results
6. This data becomes your **fundraising weapon**

### Regulatory Path (India)
- In India, health apps are regulated by **CDSCO (Central Drugs Standard Control Organisation)**
- A *decision support tool* (giving suggestions, not prescriptions) has lighter regulation than a medical device
- File under **Medical Devices Rules 2017** — consult a regulatory consultant (~₹50,000)
- Aim for **ISO 13485** certification eventually (international credibility)
